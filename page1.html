ABOUT US

What initially started as the clothing line Tees By Cooper, a Rockhampton fashion label, has transformed into a new business. Printing shirts for other local businesses, for events or their business uniforms etc. We saw a need in the market for affordable, quality T-shirt printing with a quick turnaround.  

We are passionate about creating and producing work that makes our clients go wow!Thats why we pride ourselves on a consultation approach, guiding you through the whole journey from start to finish. First we collect all the information that we need to get the job done, from Budget and quantity, too when you need the shirts. We then take all of those details and quote the job tailored to your specific needs.

Our final step is drafting up the logo on the shirt, so you can see it digitally and approve it. We then start printing! Giving you an Express Turnaround, having your shirts printed within 10 working days from the time of Confirming the Prints, Offering Premium Quality, we take the utmost care in delivering the highest quality printing for you, all at an affordable and competitive price.
